Project Nebula

Nebula is an attempt to automate the operations of a fund manager. Its a framework which combines relevant & high quality share data with investment guidelines.

The motivation for this project lies in our belief that when it comes to long-term investing, discipline can overpower genius.

"A Nebula is a vast cloud of ionized gas and cosmic dust in space, often serving as a nursery for the formation of stars and planetary systems." — Adapted from Wikipedia. It essentially represents the formation of something valuable from a large hunk of randomness. It holds the potential for greatness beyond comprehension — much like an investment portfolio.
